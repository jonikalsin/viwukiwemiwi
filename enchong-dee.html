Enchong Dee – Biography

Enchong Dee was born in Naga, Philippines on November 5, 1988. After joining ABS-CBN’s Star Magic team, this Filipino performer starred in such television series as Katorse and Ina, Kapatid, and Anak.

For his role in the Star Cinema film Sa ‘yo Lamang, he earned both Gawad PASADO and Star awards. He and fellow Filipino actor Enrique Gil appeared together in a 2012 movie called The Reunion.

Early in his career, he appeared in television advertisements for a Philippine fashion line called Bench and for the Kentucky Fried Chicken fast-food chain.
