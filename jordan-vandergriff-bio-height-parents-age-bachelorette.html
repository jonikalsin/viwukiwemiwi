Jordan Vandergriff Job

Jordan Vandergriff is a professional race car driver who’s been a part of Bob Vandergriff Racing ever since February 2018.

This NHRA Top Fuel driver and crewman made his NHRA debut in Top Fuel at the Arizona Nationals in 2019. That year, he competed in 11 events and even made it to the semifinals in Phoenix and his first final round in Dallas.

Unfortunately, despite showing promises, at the end of the 2019 season, Jordan lost his primary sponsor, D.A. Lubricants, and he had to go on the search for a new marketing partner. But this only made him stronger and believe in himself a little more.

The star was well-schooled in the process of building business-to-business relationships as a third-generation involved in the family’s Hedman business. Now, he believes that modern-day racing is a lot different than it was a racing generation ago.

As for his education, he went to Brea Olinda High School and later attended W.P. Carey School of Business – Arizona State University.
