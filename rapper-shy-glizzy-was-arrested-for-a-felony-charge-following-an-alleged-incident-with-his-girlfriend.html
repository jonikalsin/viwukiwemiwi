Rapper Shy Glizzy was arrested for a felony charge following an alleged incident with his girlfriend


Rapper Shy Glizzy allegedly pulled a gun on his girlfriend and threatened her, resulting in his arrest over the weekend. 

According to law enforcement sources cited by TMZ, the rapper was taken into custody on Saturday, May 13, after a verbal altercation with his on-again, off-again girlfriend escalated to the point where he grabbed a firearm and made a threat. 

The alleged incident took place on a public street around 9 AM on Saturday, following which Shy left the scene. West Hollywood Sheriff’s Deputies intervened, filed a criminal report, and later apprehended Shy around 6 PM on the same day. 

Shy Glizzy was booked on a felony charge of making criminal threats and was released on a $50,000 bond after spending approximately 4 hours in custody. Records showed that no gun was found on Shy during his arrest, and he denied threatening his girlfriend, with whom he has been in an on-and-off relationship for 4 months. 

The police continue to investigate the case.
