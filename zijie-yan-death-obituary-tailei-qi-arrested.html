Zijie Yan was a 38-year-old Assistant Professor at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. He began working at the University in June 2019 and was loved by many.

Before that, he was an assistant professor at Clarkson University from August 2015 to June 2019. Likewise, Yan was a Postdoctoral Scholar at the University of Chicago between October 2011 and July 2015.

Furthermore, Yan’s research interests included optical Trapping and Manipulation, Microfluidics, Holography, and Electronic and Photonics Nanomaterials.

His name came into the media prominence when his death was shared following a deadly shooting on Monday. 

Zijie Yan Death Shocked The World

Zijie Yan death came as a shock to many people as he was shot and killed in Monday’s on-campus shooting. Tailei Qi is the person who shot and killed Yan.

Furthermore, it is still too early to establish a definitive motive. While Qi’s car was found on campus and seized, the firearm used in the shooting has not yet been recovered.

Tailei Qi Arrested For Shooting And Killing UNC Chapel Hill Professor
