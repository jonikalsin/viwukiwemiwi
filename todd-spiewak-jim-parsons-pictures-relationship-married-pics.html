Todd Spiewak &amp; Jim Parsons Pictures See Photos Of The Couple Hollywood Life

Jim Parsons and Todd Spiewak met all the way back in November 2002 and were united by Spiewak’s boss and Parsons’ best friend. Ever since that blind date they were attached at the hip for 15 whole years until finally deciding to take their relationship to the next level.

On May 13, 2017, the couple fittingly invited close friends and family to the historic Rainbow Room in NYC to have a celebration and say their vows. 

Their strong marriage isn’t their biggest achievement though!
