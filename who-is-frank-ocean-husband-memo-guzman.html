Born Christopher Edwin Breaux on October 28, 1987, Christopher Francis “Frank” Ocean is an American singer, songwriter, and rapper. Music critics have praised his works for their avant-garde aesthetics and reflective, elliptical lyrics.

He and his family moved to the New Orleans region when he was five years old. He was brought up as a Christian and even spent some time as a practicing Catholic.

Ocean enrolled at the University of New Orleans to study English after completing his high school education at John Ehret High School in Marrero, Louisiana, in 2005.

Hurricane Katrina, which ravaged New Orleans in August 2005, compelled him to switch to the University of Louisiana at Lafayette after damaging his house and recording studio. He remained there for a short while before leaving to concentrate on his music career.
