Career and Professional Life

Professionally, King of the 4th Quarter is a social media personality. He is primarily a YouTube gamer widely known for his King of the 4th Quarter (KOT4Q) channel. He has gained popularity there for his NBA 2K Let’s Plays as well as NBA-themed vlog entries. Moreover, he began his YouTube channel on June 14, 2011.

At present, the 4th Quarter has amassed more than 852,000 subscribers to his YouTube channel since its beginning.
